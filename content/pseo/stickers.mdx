## Sticker shops live or die on the listing fee

Stickers are the textbook case of a category where Etsy's fee structure punishes the price point you naturally land on. The average Etsy sticker pack sells for $3.50–8, which puts every order squarely in the danger zone where flat fees consume a disproportionate share of revenue.

The category itself is healthy — sticker shops scale fast on TikTok and Pinterest, repeat purchase rates are high, and inventory is cheap. The trap is purely mathematical.

## Why a $6 sticker pack barely breaks even

A typical sticker pack: $6 sale + $1.50 shipping = $7.50 gross. Production cost ~$0.85, postage ~$1.40. Etsy's fees:

- Listing fee: $0.20
- Transaction fee (6.5% of $7.50): $0.49
- Payment processing (3% + $0.25): $0.48
- Off-Site Ads at 15%: $1.13

Total Etsy fees: **$2.30 on $7.50 in revenue.** That's a **30.7% effective fee rate.** After production and shipping cost ($2.25 combined), net profit is **$2.95 — about 49% margin** on the item.

It looks okay on the spreadsheet. But sticker shops only succeed at volume — 500+ orders per month — and your customer service, packaging time, and ad creation time scale with order count, not with revenue.

## The common mistake: flat-rate shipping that loses money

Sticker sellers often charge "$1.50 flat shipping" because the math seems clean. Actual shipping with envelope and protective backing is closer to $1.40 — basically break-even. The problem is when the buyer is rural, international, or the item gets returned: shipping cost can jump to $3-4 and you've already locked in $1.50.

The other failure: not bundling. A 5-pack at $18 carries the same flat fees as a single $4 sheet. Your effective fee rate drops from 30% to 15%, and your packaging time per dollar of revenue collapses.

## How to fix it

- **Push toward $10–15 average order value** through 3-pack and 5-pack bundles.
- **Charge actual shipping**, not flat rate, unless you've measured your real average across a few hundred orders.
- **Opt out of Off-Site Ads** while you're under $10k. Sticker buyers come from social, not Etsy search ads.
- **Holographic and special-finish stickers** can command 2–3× the standard price for similar production cost — the highest-leverage move in the category.
