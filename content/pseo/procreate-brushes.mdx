## Procreate brushes: the high-margin digital sweet spot

Procreate brush packs sit in a comfortable price band — $8 to $20 — that's higher than coloring pages or single stickers but lower than full course bundles. That's the price point where the flat fees stop dominating but percentage fees haven't yet eaten the seller alive. The headline math looks great. The catch is that this category is increasingly crowded, and the SEO and discovery side gets harder every quarter.

## A $12 brush pack sale, fully decomposed

$12 pack, no shipping, no cost of goods. Etsy fees:

- Listing fee: $0.20
- Transaction fee (6.5% of $12): $0.78
- Payment processing (3% + $0.25): $0.61
- Off-Site Ads at 15%: $1.80

Baseline: **$1.59 — 13.3% of revenue.** With Off-Site Ads: **$3.39 — 28.3% of revenue.** Net profit: **$10.41 baseline, $8.61 with Off-Site Ads.**

Compared to a $4 single sticker (where flat fees alone consume 11%), a $12 brush pack is a much more forgiving price point. The flat $0.45 is 3.75% of revenue here — small enough to fade into the percentage-fee noise. That's why every digital category benchmarks itself against the "$10 floor": below it, fees punish you; above it, they normalize.

## The common mistake: pricing the second pack like the first

New brush sellers often launch a single $12 pack and then a second $12 pack as "more of the same" — and lose to bundled competitors. The buyer who bought the first pack will pay $24 for a "complete set" but won't buy the second pack at $12 unless it's actively different. Single-pack repeat purchases are rare in this category.

The other mistake: pricing too low to "compete." Procreate buyers care more about quality, style, and reviews than $2 of savings. A $7 brush pack signals commodity; a $15 pack signals craft. Buyers in this niche self-select on price.

## How to fix it

- **Build a "complete set" tier.** A $24–32 multi-pack bundle is the durable hit, not the single $12 pack.
- **Free brush as a lead magnet.** One genuinely useful free brush in your shop drives reviews and follower growth, which compounds in Etsy's algorithm.
- **Opt out of Off-Site Ads under $10k.** Procreate audiences come from Instagram and Pinterest organically. The 15% Etsy attribution rarely justifies itself.
- **Demo videos in the listing.** This is the single highest-leverage listing change. Static screenshots lose to anyone who shows the brush in motion.
- **Off-Etsy: Gumroad + Patreon.** A $12 pack on Gumroad nets ~$11. A $12 pack on Etsy with Off-Site Ads nets $8.61. Same buyer; the Etsy channel is paying you 22% less.
