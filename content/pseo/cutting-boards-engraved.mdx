## Engraved cutting boards: oversized shipping is the killer

Personalized engraved cutting boards (laser-engraved or CNC-routed walnut, maple, bamboo) sit at the intersection of every difficult Etsy economic: oversized package dimensions, weight-driven shipping costs, real material cost, and a buyer who's price-anchored on $40–50 retail despite the production complexity. The wood is the smallest line item. Shipping the finished board without damaging it is what actually eats the margin.

## A $42 engraved cutting board sale, fully decomposed

$42 board + $12 shipping = $54 gross. Walnut or maple blank $14 (mid-size 12×18 cut from hardwood lumber), engraving consumables and labor $0, real shipping $11 (Priority Mail Cubic-Large, 4–6 lbs). Total cost ~$25. Etsy fees on the gross:

- Listing fee: $0.20
- Transaction fee (6.5% of $54): $3.51
- Payment processing (3% + $0.25): $1.87
- Off-Site Ads at 15%: $8.10

Etsy total: **$13.68 — 25.3% of gross.** After cost of goods and shipping: net profit is **$54 − $25 − $13.68 = $15.32 — about 28.4% net margin** on the buyer-facing total.

At 30 minutes of total labor per board (laser file prep + engraving runtime + finish/oil + packing), hourly rate works out to $30.64. That's the highest-margin handmade home category in this audit — but only because the shipping was budgeted realistically. Sellers who underprice shipping go negative on this category fast.

## The common mistake: undercharging for shipping

The single most common failure mode in engraved cutting boards is treating shipping as a $5–6 expense when it's actually $11–14 dimensional. The seller eats the difference, and the listing nets $3–5 instead of $15. This sounds avoidable; it isn't, because Etsy's "free shipping" preference pushes sellers toward absorbing it into the item price — which works only if the item price was set high enough to absorb it.

The other failure: not differentiating wood species. A walnut cutting board commands a $15–20 premium over maple at the same dimensions, with roughly $4 difference in wood cost. Walnut buyers know the species; commodity-pricing your walnut piece is leaving real money on the table.

## How to fix it

- **Price $52–68 with free shipping baked in.** The buyer perceives "free shipping" as a positive signal even though the cost is built in. The total to them is identical to $42 + $12 separately, and Etsy weights the "free shipping" listing higher.
- **Wood species in the title explicitly.** "Walnut engraved cutting board" beats "engraved cutting board" on both search intent and conversion.
- **Personalization upcharge tier.** A $52 standard board, $72 with extensive personalization (family last name + established date + custom symbol). The marginal labor is small; the marginal margin is real.
- **Use the $100 Off-Site Ads cap.** Premium boards above $200 effectively pay sub-15% on the Off-Site Ads line. Wedding-gift bundling is a path to that price tier.
- **Pair with a cheese board / serving board bundle.** Two-piece sets at $98 are the highest-AOV listings in this category and the fee math is materially better.
