## Bath salts: heavy shipping, tight margins

Epsom salt is one of the cheapest raw materials in handmade beauty — about $1.50 per pound bulk. Essential oils and carrier oils add maybe $1–2 per jar. The killer in this category is shipping weight. A 12oz jar of bath salts is heavy, and Priority Mail rates scale by weight. Etsy fees apply to the gross including the heavy shipping charge. The result is a category that looks like 70% margin on paper and clears 25–30% in reality.

## A $14 bath salt jar sale, fully decomposed

$14 jar + $5 shipping = $19 gross. Epsom salt $1.50, essential oil $1.25, dried botanicals $0.75, jar + lid $1.25, label $0.30, real shipping $4.75. Total cost ~$9.80. Etsy fees on the gross:

- Listing fee: $0.20
- Transaction fee (6.5% of $19): $1.24
- Payment processing (3% + $0.25): $0.82
- Off-Site Ads at 15%: $2.85

Etsy total: **$5.11 — 26.9% of gross.** After cost of goods and shipping: net profit is **$19 − $9.80 − $5.11 = $4.09 — about 21.5% net margin** on the buyer-facing total.

The 21.5% margin is the structural ceiling on single-jar bath salts. Shops that try to push it higher run into the price ceiling — buyers won't pay $24 for a single jar of what they perceive as flavored Epsom salt. The path to better economics is multi-jar bundling and gift sets, not higher single-unit prices.

## The common mistake: ignoring weight in shipping calc

The most common failure in this category is using a flat $4 shipping rate without checking actual USPS pricing on a 12oz jar to zone 8. The real shipping cost is $7–9 in many destinations. The seller eats the difference and the listing nets $1–2 instead of $4. This is an avoidable error caught with a 2-minute USPS zone check.

The other failure: glass jars vs. plastic. Glass jars look premium but break in shipping ~5–8% of the time. The refund cost destroys the per-jar economics. Most successful bath salt shops use BPA-free plastic jars or refillable kraft tubes.

## How to fix it

- **3-jar gift sets.** $34 for three variety scents. Same flat fees as one $14 jar but 2.4× the revenue. The fee math drops from 26.9% to under 22%.
- **Use kraft tubes or PETG jars.** Lower breakage rate, similar buyer perception, less packaging cushioning needed.
- **Wholesale to local spas / Airbnbs.** A 24-jar wholesale order at $7 each ($168) avoids Etsy's fee stack and pays the seller materially more per jar than retail.
- **Heavy-package shipping calibration.** Use Etsy's Shipping Profiles with explicit zone-based rates. Don't eat the cost of buyers in distant zones.
- **Premium ingredient framing.** "Dead Sea salt" or "Himalayan pink salt" variants command $4–6 more retail than Epsom-only at $0.50–1 marginal cost. Pure margin upside.
