## Canvas prints: bulky shipping, supplier squeeze

Stretched canvas prints are POD-style art products that sit in a difficult margin position on Etsy. Print suppliers (Printify, Printful, Gelato) charge $15–22 per stretched canvas at standard sizes. Shipping is genuinely expensive — these are oversized, can't-bend boxes — and the supplier often charges the seller for shipping while Etsy fees apply to the buyer-facing total. The result is a category where the seller's actual take-home is meaningfully lower than the headline gross suggests.

## A $48 canvas print sale, fully decomposed

$48 canvas + $11 shipping = $59 gross. Supplier cost $18 (16×20 stretched canvas, print-on-demand), real shipping $10.50 (charged through by supplier). Total cost ~$28.50. Etsy fees on the gross:

- Listing fee: $0.20
- Transaction fee (6.5% of $59): $3.84
- Payment processing (3% + $0.25): $2.02
- Off-Site Ads at 15%: $8.85

Etsy total: **$14.91 — 25.3% of gross.** After supplier cost and shipping: net profit is **$59 − $28.50 − $14.91 = $15.59 — about 26.4% net margin** on the buyer-facing total.

At $0 labor per sale (POD fulfillment), the margin per order is the actual margin. The category lives or dies on volume and whether the supplier's quality matches the listing's promise.

## The common mistake: matching mass-market canvas pricing

New canvas print sellers benchmark against Wayfair or Society6 retail ($24–38 for 16×20 prints) and price too low. The Etsy buyer searching for handmade or custom art is paying for differentiation, not price. A $58 canvas with a strong aesthetic outsells a $42 canvas of equivalent supplier quality because the price signals "real artist."

The other failure: not accounting for return rates. Canvas prints have higher return rates than most Etsy categories (5–8% vs. 1–3% for digital products) because color reproduction varies between monitor and print. Sellers who don't budget for returns operate at a smaller effective margin than they think.

## How to fix it

- **Price $58–88 for 16×20 canvas.** This is the durable Etsy price range. Matching Wayfair's mass-market pricing is a race to zero.
- **Multi-size variant listings.** Same design at 11×14 ($38), 16×20 ($58), 24×30 ($98). The buyer chooses size, the seller captures every conversion the design earns.
- **Triptych / set listings.** A 3-piece set at $148 sells well as gallery wall styling and has materially better fee math than three single-piece sales.
- **Use the $100 Off-Site Ads cap aggressively.** A $400 large canvas attributed to Off-Site Ads pays $100 (cap), effectively 25% on that line — but for items above $667, the effective rate drops below 15%.
- **Direct supplier relationship.** If volume justifies it, moving from Printify to a direct local print supplier can save $4–6 per unit. At 200 sales/month that's $800–1,200 of pure margin.
