## Friendship bracelets: $15 list, double-digit fee rate

Friendship bracelets and embroidery-thread bracelets are the entry-level handmade jewelry category on Etsy — low material cost, simple skill ceiling, infinite design possibility. The unit economics look fantastic at first glance and then deteriorate the moment you do the actual math at scale. Each $15 sale loses roughly 15% to fees baseline; at Off-Site Ads attribution, that's 27%+. Volume cures some of it; bundling cures more.

## A $15 friendship bracelet sale, fully decomposed

$15 bracelet + $3 shipping = $18 gross. Materials $4 (embroidery thread + clasp/beads), real shipping $2.75. Total cost ~$6.75. Etsy fees on the gross:

- Listing fee: $0.20
- Transaction fee (6.5% of $18): $1.17
- Payment processing (3% + $0.25): $0.79
- Off-Site Ads at 15%: $2.70

Etsy total: **$4.86 — 27% of gross.** After cost of goods and shipping: net profit is **$18 − $6.75 − $4.86 = $6.39 — about 35.5% net margin** on the buyer-facing total.

That 35.5% looks fine until you factor labor. A custom-pattern friendship bracelet takes 30–60 minutes to weave. At 45 minutes per piece the hourly rate is about $8.52 — well below minimum wage. This is a hobby economy unless you fundamentally restructure the listing.

## The common mistake: per-bracelet listings

Most friendship bracelet shops list each color combo as a $12–18 standalone product. Each sale clears ~$6 after fees and the labor doesn't fit into the math. To clear $1,000/month at $6.39 net per sale you need 156 individual orders — every flat fee compounds.

The shops that work in this category sell _stacks_. A $32 5-bracelet stack has the same materials per bracelet but absorbs fees on a single $32 gross. The fee math is dramatically better, the customer gets a coordinated set, and the labor is the same total time as 5 separate orders.

## How to fix it

- **Stacks, not singles.** 3-stack at $24, 5-stack at $38, 7-stack at $52. The buyer who would have bought one wants three; the seller's per-bracelet economics flip.
- **Pre-built color stories.** "Summer pastel stack," "earth-tone stack," "neon stack" converts dramatically better than "pick your colors" custom listings. Customization is friction.
- **Subscription friendship bracelet box.** A $20/month "new stack each month" subscription on Subbly or Cratejoy keeps recurring revenue at 100% margin. Etsy is the acquisition channel; subscription is the lifetime-value channel.
- **Opt out of Off-Site Ads under $10k.** Friendship bracelet buyers find shops through TikTok and Instagram organically. The 15% Etsy attribution is dead weight.
- **TikTok-native content.** This category is unusually well-suited to ASMR weaving videos and stack-styling content. The shops that scale do this; the shops that don't, stay small.
